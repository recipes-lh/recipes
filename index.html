Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Serves: 2  •  Time: 15 min

Ingredients: 200g spaghetti, 3 tbsp olive oil, 3 cloves garlic (sliced), pinch chili flakes, salt, parsley, lemon zest.

Steps: Cook pasta in salted water. Sauté garlic in oil until just golden; add chili. Toss pasta with pan oil, splash pasta water, season. Finish with parsley and zest.

Tip: Save pasta water; it’s the sauce.
